Track Weightings

Students are randomly assigned a track with a probability calculated by your specified weights. Weights need be integers that are at least 1 and no greater than 1000.

Example: if Track A has weight "5" and Track B has weight "3", then the total weight is 8, and students have a 5/8 probability of getting assigned Track A and 3/8 probabilty getting assigned Track B.

Example: if Track A has weight "30" and Track B has weight "70", then the total weight is 100, and students have a "30/100" probability of getting assigned Track A and "70/100" probability getting assigned Track B.
